Welcome to the Battlefield Stats page!

This website consists of player records scraped from Battlefield Tracker websites for Battlefield 1, Battlefield 4, and Battlefield Hardline. The data were scraped from Battlefield Tracker leaderboards, sorted by Game Score, at the following websites respectively: https://battlefieldtracker.com/bf1/leaderboards/all/Score, https://battlefieldtracker.com/bf4/leaderboards/all, and https://battlefieldtracker.com/bfh/leaderboards/all. You can find the collection of records in their respective tabs.

Player records on each tab are filterable by the filters at the top of each game tab. You can also click on each player record to see the detailed record for that particular player.

The "Data Analytics" tab allows you to generate various plots of the relationship between any two numeric variables that were scraped, with a variety of filters on how the graph should be generated.

Enjoy!
